PHISHING INDICATORS IDENTIFIED:
====================================================================

1. SUSPICIOUS SENDER DOMAIN
   - Domain: secure-verification-center.com
   - Not affiliated with any legitimate organization
   - Recently registered (WHOIS check recommended)

2. URGENCY TACTICS
   - "24 hours" artificial deadline
   - Threat of "permanent suspension"
   - Designed to bypass rational thinking

3. MALICIOUS LINK
   - URL: secure-account-verify.com
   - Typosquatting/fake domain
   - Leads to credential harvesting page

4. GENERIC GREETING
   - "Dear Valued Customer" (not personalized)
   - No specific account details
   - Mass-distribution indicator

5. FEAR TACTICS
   - References "Nigeria" to create alarm
   - "Suspicious activity" claim
   - Pressure to act without verification

====================================================================
ANALYSIS NOTES:
====================================================================

This is a classic credential phishing attack. The attacker creates
a fake login page that captures usernames and passwords when victims
attempt to "verify" their accounts.

RECOMMENDED ACTION:
- DO NOT click any links
- DO NOT enter credentials
- Report to IT security immediately
- Delete the email
- Verify account status through official channels only
